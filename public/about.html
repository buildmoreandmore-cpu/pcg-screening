Our History

PCG Screening Services, LLC was established in 2003, specializing in employment, tenant, and non-profit background checks. The company expanded from small Chamber of Commerce registrations to serving corporations nationwide. We provide HR consulting and hold certifications including WBENC (woman-owned business), SHRM membership, and FCRA certification through NAPBS.
